About the CHURCH MANDATE

Jesus gave the Mandate "Go ye therefore and teach all nations baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, teaching them to observe all things, whatsoever I have commanded you; and I am with you always even unto the end of the world(age) Amen

It is the Mandate above that is generally referred to as the great commission

-This great commission is all about making disciples in Christ

-We must recognize that this mandate is not only about leading people to Christ but much more importantly about teaching of kingdom principles and mysteries that will establish men in christ and in faith; enabling them to be true reflection of christ

"And wisdom and knowledge shall be the stability of thy time and strength of salvation: The fear of the Lord is His treasure"

Isaiah 33:6
